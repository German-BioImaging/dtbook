Model#

The current model used in the DTDP is to collect all of the properties from the key-value pairs in the IDR and attach them to the image via a b-node using the “depicts” predicate:

:SomeImage is a Wikidata:Image that depicts something

  • with a gene symbol

  • with a medical condition

  • with age at event

  • and sex.
